Goals

Motivation

eOS aims to provide a standardized, secure, and maintainable Linux-based operating system for the EU public sector. The motivation behind the project is to:

  • Provide a common base OS for EU public organizations with the ability to add specific layers.
  • Enable a unified desktop environment for easier management and interoperability.
  • Improve transparency, reduce costs, and promote open standards.
  • Foster collaboration across European public administrations and free software communities.

Next Steps

  • Develop an atomic base OS with layered architecture for modular deployment.
  • Test installations on various public sector devices.
  • Document user, software, and device management processes.
  • Engage early adopter organizations for proof-of-concept deployment.
  • Encourage hosting and support on official EU code platforms.

Project Scope

eOS focuses on deploying a Linux-based OS in typical public sector organizations. Out of scope:

  • Developing a new Linux distribution from scratch.
  • Deployment outside public/corporate environments.
  • Deployment on smartphones, tablets, or non-standard devices.
  • Emulating Windows or Android applications.

For demonstration, Firefox and LibreOffice may be included in the proof-of-concept setup.
